Privacy and Safety

Drafts, reader preferences, likes, library data, and profile settings are stored locally in this version. That means your changes stay in this browser until a Firebase sync pass is added later, which is a good final step once the interface and overall experience feel complete enough to lock down.

About VividReads

VividReads is a premium reading and writing platform built to make discovering books, saving favorites, and creating original stories feel beautiful, immersive, and intentional. The vision is to bring together strong reading comfort, a social community feel, and a polished writer studio inside one experience with a dark electric identity that stands apart from generic book apps.

Terms and Conditions: by using VividReads, readers and writers agree to use the platform respectfully, avoid harmful behavior, and only publish content they have the right to share.

Code of Conduct: treat other readers and writers with respect, do not harass, threaten, impersonate, or abuse anyone, and use comments and replies in a constructive way.

Terms of Use: VividReads is for reading, writing, discovering, and engaging with stories. Content, profiles, comments, and books should be used in ways that support the platform’s creative purpose rather than damage it.

Help and Support

VividReads should feel welcoming to both readers and writers, so support needs to be richer than a single short note. A stronger support area can eventually include reading help, writing and publishing guidance, reporting tools, account questions, copyright guidance, community safety information, and ways to contact support for technical issues. For now, this version keeps the guidance practical: keep backup copies before major upgrades, test each build before publishing, and move to cloud sync once the core experience feels final enough to lock in across devices.
